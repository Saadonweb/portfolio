Features

Everything guilt
has to offer.

Four ways TabGhost makes you confront your tab hoarding problem.

☠️
Tab Graveyard

Every tab you close gets a proper burial. See exactly which sites died in your care, when they were last visited, and how long they survived your neglect.

⚡
Resurrection Mode

Changed your mind? Hit "↑ Rise" and watch a forgotten tab crawl back from the dead. It's like a second chance — but for websites.

💀
Guilt Score

A real-time score from 0 to 100 measuring how bad of a tab hoarder you are. 0 means you're suspiciously productive. 100 means your RAM has filed a restraining order.

📊
Weekly Guilt Report

Every week, a notification arrives with a brutally honest summary of your tab habits. Abandoned count, resurrection rate, and a personalised verdict you probably deserve.

How It Works

Three steps to
face your guilt.

01
🔍
TabGhost Watches

Silently runs in the background, logging every tab you visit with a timestamp. No RAM hit, no performance drag.

02
💀
Tabs Become Ghosts

When a tab goes 3+ days without a visit, it earns ghost status. Close it and it moves to the Graveyard — forever remembered.

03
⚡
You Face the Truth

Open the popup anytime to see your graveyard, guilt score, and weekly report. Resurrect what matters. Bury the rest.
